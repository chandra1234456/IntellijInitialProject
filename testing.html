Android Testing Interview Questions

1. JUnit 4 Interview Questions

  • What is JUnit 4 and why is it used in Android testing?
  • What are the key annotations used in JUnit 4 (@Test, @Before, @After)?
  • Difference between assertEquals, assertTrue, and assertFalse?
  • What is the role of test runners in JUnit 4?
  • How do you handle exceptions in JUnit 4 test cases?
  • What is the difference between unit tests and instrumentation tests?

2. Mockito Interview Questions

  • What is Mockito and why is it used?
  • What is the difference between mock(), spy(), and @Mock?
  • What is stubbing in Mockito?
  • How do you verify method calls using Mockito?
  • What is ArgumentCaptor used for?
  • How does Mockito help in isolating dependencies in unit testing?

3. Espresso Interview Questions

  • What is Espresso and why is it used in Android UI testing?
  • What are the main components of Espresso?
  • What is ViewMatchers, ViewActions, and ViewAssertions?
  • How does Espresso handle UI synchronization automatically?
  • What is the difference between Espresso and UI Automator?
  • How do you test RecyclerView using Espresso?

4. UI Automator Interview Questions

  • What is UI Automator used for in Android testing?
  • How is UI Automator different from Espresso?
  • What are UiDevice and UiSelector?
  • Can UI Automator test multiple apps? Explain.
  • How do you automate system UI components like notifications or settings?
  • What are the limitations of UI Automator?

5. Mixed / Scenario-Based Questions

  • When would you choose JUnit 4 over instrumentation tests?
  • How do Mockito and JUnit 4 work together?
  • When should you prefer Espresso vs UI Automator?
  • How do you structure a complete Android testing pyramid?
  • Explain a real project where you used all these testing tools.
